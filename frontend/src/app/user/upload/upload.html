Introduction to Pneumonia Types

Pneumonia is an infection that inflames the air sacs in one or both lungs. The air sacs may fill with fluid or pus, causing cough with phlegm or pus, fever, chills, and difficulty breathing. Pneumonia can be classified into several types.

This classifier is focused on detecting the two most common types of pneumonia: viral and bacterial.
